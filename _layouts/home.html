How the network is built

  1. The Markdown files in _publications/ are parsed and stripped of footnotes, headings, links, code, and bibliographies.
  2. Italian abstracts are machine-translated to English with Helsinki-NLP/opus-mt-it-en and cached on disk.
  3. Each cleaned title + abstract is embedded as a 768-dimensional vector by the BAAI/bge-base-en-v1.5 model.
  4. Pairwise cosine similarity yields a dense matrix over every pair of papers.
  5. An edge is drawn from each paper to its single strongest neighbour, unless even that best score falls below 0.70, in which case the paper stays unconnected.
  6. A D3 force simulation lays out the graph with link, repulsion, gravity, and collision forces; the arrangement is pre-computed offline so it loads already settled.
